About

I believe developing products is an inquisitive journey in an ambiguous and uncertain world (VUCA). I enjoy solving complex problems and the investigative process to understand human needs, motivations, desires, and the mental models that shape our perception.

My strengths encompass defining product strategy, user research, and designing seamless, human-centered experiences. I thrive on building multidisciplinary teams, solving business problems, and envisioning experiences that delight users.
